About Me

I'm a PhD Student currently working on Discrete Mathematics in the US. Originally I'm from India where I was born and brought up. If you're interested in math, do check out my academic webpage as well!

I have been watching anime for a bit over 10 years now, and that's where I started picking up my Japanese from. After that I kept trying to read VNs and LNs, and that is how I learned this language. This means that these translations are the blabberings of a person with no formal education in Japanese, and hence can be flawed. Please read at your own discretion!

There's not really much else to say about myself. If I suddenly disappear for long periods of time, that probably means that I'm busy with RL and academic stuff. I plan on posting twitter updates in these situations, so keep an eye out for that! :)

Thank you for coming over to read my translations.
