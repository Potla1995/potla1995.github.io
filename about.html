About Me

I'm a PhD Student currently working on Discrete Mathematics at the University of Illinois at Chicago, USA. Born and brought up in India. If you're interested in math, do check out my academic webpage!

Anime has been one of my favourite pastimes for a bit over 10 years now, and that's where I started picking up my Japanese from. Around five years ago VNs and LNs also happened, and so that is where my knowledge of Japanese is based on. This means that the translations you'll find in OTL.html are the blabberings of a person with no formal education in Japanese, and hence can be flawed. Please read at your own discretion!

There's not really much else to say about myself. If I suddenly disappear for long periods of time, it probably means that I'm busy with RL and academic stuff. I plan on posting twitter updates in these situations, so keep an eye out for that! :)

Thank you for coming over to read my translations.
